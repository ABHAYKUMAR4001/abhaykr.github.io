About Me

Hi, I’m Abhay Kumar, an accomplished engineer with a proven track record spanning automotive, rail transport and healthcare industries. My journey has been defined by designing complex systems, leading multifunctional teams and delivering high‑quality, safety‑critical solutions. As a lifelong learner and educator, I also enjoy teaching mathematics and physics and mentoring aspiring engineers.

I currently work at Jaguar Land Rover as a Senior Engineer in ADAS Software Development & Requirements, where I lead cross‑functional teams to build software defined vehicles and next‑generation driver assistance features. Prior to this, I gained rich experience in system interoperability at Baxter, digital cockpit and ADAS systems at Harman and passenger announcement & communication systems at Alstom. These roles have honed my ability to bridge customer requirements with engineering execution.

Beyond engineering, I’m passionate about digital marketing, web development, painting and contributing to education platforms. I hold a B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from the National Institute of Technology, Srinagar and a Post Graduate Diploma in Information and Cyber Security from Bangalore Institute of Technology – IGNOU.
